About Hawkins Construction

A Uganda based building and civil works contractor, delivering quality construction and reliable supply since 2022.

Our story

Built on trust and technical depth.

Hawkins Construction and General Supplies Ltd was established in 2022 and operates from Wampewo, Kasangati Town Council in Wakiso District. We serve private and commercial clients across Uganda.

We bring together an experienced leadership team, skilled trades and a dependable supply network to deliver projects that hold up to scrutiny. Our work spans fuel and commercial infrastructure, building construction, property development and construction materials supply.

Our direction

Vision and mission.

Vision

To be Uganda's most trusted name in construction and supply.

We aim to set the standard for reliability and craftsmanship, becoming the partner of choice for clients who cannot afford to get it wrong.

Mission

To deliver dependable construction and supply solutions that create lasting value.

We combine sound engineering, disciplined project management and honest pricing to build infrastructure that serves communities and businesses.

What guides us

Core values.

  • Excellence

  • Integrity

    We do what we say, price fairly and stand behind our work.

  • Safety

  • Reliability

  • Innovation

  • Customer satisfaction
